Frequently Asked Questions

What types of cameras do you repair?

We repair DSLR, mirrorless, compact, film, and professional video cameras from all major brands.

Do you use genuine replacement parts?

Yes, we use genuine parts from original manufacturers whenever available to ensure quality and compatibility.

How long does a typical repair take?

Most repairs are completed within 2-5 business days. Complex repairs or rare parts may take longer, but we always keep you updated.

Can I get a free estimate?

Yes! Bring your equipment to our shop or contact us online for a free, no-obligation estimate.

Do you offer warranty on repairs?

All our repairs come with a 90-day warranty covering parts and labor for your peace of mind.

Can you repair water-damaged cameras?

We can often repair water-damaged cameras, depending on the extent of the damage. Bring it in for a free assessment.

Is mail-in service available?

Yes, you can mail your equipment to us for repair. Contact us for instructions and shipping details.

How do I know if my camera is worth repairing?

Our technicians will inspect your camera and give honest advice on whether repair is cost-effective or if replacement is a better option.
